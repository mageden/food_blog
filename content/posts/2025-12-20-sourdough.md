Process notes
- Levain looked slightly past prime, concave on top
- 78F beginning of autolyse, 30m
- 74F beginning bulk, 78 fold 1, ...
- Stopped at 3.5h, dough was sticky and hard to preshape. Likely overproofed. Then let rest for 15m
- Shaping was also sticky and weak, another sign of overproofed bulk

Result notes
- Taste was acidic, crumb was slightly gummy and moist. Overall flavor was good
- I think that acidity and overproofing the bulk were the problems. The levain needs to be young not overly old
- Weak oven spring
- Good ear, but too far on one side and not centered
- Too much rice flour, dinished final appearance

Next time
- Be careful about levain not being overly ripe, want to use "young levain"
- Use mixture of rice flour and wheat flour for dusting basket and be more conservative

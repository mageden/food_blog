        - "12g orange blossom water (1 Tbsp)"
        - "9g salt (1 1/2 tsp)"
        - "3g ground anise (1 tsp)"
        - "Zest from 1 orange"
        - "36g orange juice (3 Tbsp)"
        - "120g olive oil (1/2 cup + 1 Tbsp)"
        - "500g flour (3 3/4 cups)"
        - "7g instant dry yeast (2 1/4 tsp / 1 packet)"
  step_groups:
    - steps:
        - "Make preferment with 170g water + 170g flour and pinch of yeast, let sit 12hr at room temperature."
        - "Mix sugar, orange blossom water, salt, ground anise, orange zest, orange juice, and oil (everything but flour + yeast)."
        - "Mix flour and yeast."
        - "Combine and mix for 5-8 minutes in stand mixer. It should be smooth and slightly sticky at end of mixing."
        - "Let rise until doubled (~80F), ~3.5 hours."
        - "Divide dough into 2 loaves."

## Notes

Process notes
- Waited to apply sugar after oil that was brushed on had absorbed so it didn't stick very well

Result notes
- Fluffy, citrusy, floral, excellent flavor. Slam dunk. Very rich and oily

Future notes
- Apply olive oil and then immediately roll in citrus sugar when finished

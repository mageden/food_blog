# Pane Siciliano

Bread bakers apprentice recipe

Process Notes
- Dough temp after mixing was 66F, put in Breville at 120 for 2 minutes to warm up
- Overproofed the bulk ferment despite cooler temperature. It was overproofed at 1h45m despite the proof time of 2hrs.

Results
- Excellent flavor and texture

Notes
- Switch to poolish instead of pate fermentee. It is easier to mix and has a mild sweet flavor
- It is a pain to regrigerate the dough in those large shapes. Try baking same day to compare

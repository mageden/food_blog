# Tartine bead

Process notes
- Followed method in perfect loaf
- Ambient temperature 65. Initial water mixed in at 83. Final temp was 72 after mixing levain and autolys - was aiming for 78.
- Bowl folds: 15min (77f) x4. Final temp was ~ 80f.
- Levain was substantially less than double in the morning. Put in proofer to warm up and raise for an hour until it doubled and started to crest
- The bulk ferment appeated weak so I continued for a bit after the 4 hour mark, ~30minutes. Still didn't look quite right.
- No rise after overnight proof in fridge

Thoughts
- Poor results seem likely due to the starter at this point. One explanation for the gummy texture and moist crumb in previous loaves could be too high of an acid content breaking down the gluten strength. Additionally, that would make the rise sluggish leading to reduced oven spring which I am observing.

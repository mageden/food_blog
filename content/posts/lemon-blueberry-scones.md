  These scones are perfect for a weekend morning with coffee. The lemon glaze pushes them from good to irresistible.
recipe:
  prep_time: "20 minutes"
  cook_time: "18–22 minutes"
  yield: "8 scones"
  ingredient_groups:
    - name: "Scones"
      items:
        - "2 cups all-purpose flour"
        - "1/3 cup sugar"
        - "1 tbsp baking powder"
        - "1/2 tsp salt"
        - "Zest of 1 lemon"
        - "1/2 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed"
        - "1/2 cup heavy cream"
        - "1 large egg"
        - "1 cup fresh blueberries"
    - name: "Glaze"
      items:
        - "1 cup powdered sugar"
        - "2 tbsp fresh lemon juice"
  step_groups:
    - steps:
        - "Preheat oven to **400°F** (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment."
        - "Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest."
        - "Cut in cold butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs (some pea-sized pieces are fine)."
        - "In a small bowl, whisk cream and egg. Pour into the flour mixture and stir until just combined."
        - "Gently fold in blueberries."
        - "Shape the dough into an 8-inch round, about 1 inch thick. Cut into 8 wedges."
        - "Bake **18–22 minutes** until golden."
        - "Whisk the glaze ingredients and drizzle over cooled scones."

## Tips

> Keep the butter **very cold** — that's the secret to flaky scones. If it's warm in your kitchen, pop the cubed butter in the freezer for 10 minutes before starting.

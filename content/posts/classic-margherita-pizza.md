recipe:
  prep_time: "20 minutes"
  cook_time: "8–10 minutes"
  yield: "1 pizza (12-inch)"
  ingredient_groups:
    - items:
        - "1 ball pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)"
        - "1/2 cup San Marzano tomato sauce"
        - "8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced"
        - "Fresh basil leaves"
        - "Extra-virgin olive oil"
        - "Salt and pepper"
  step_groups:
    - steps:
        - "Preheat your oven to **500°F** (260°C) with a pizza stone or inverted baking sheet inside."
        - "Stretch the dough into a 12-inch round on a floured surface."
        - "Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce, leaving a 1-inch border."
        - "Arrange mozzarella slices evenly over the sauce."
        - "Slide onto the hot stone and bake for **8–10 minutes** until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling."
        - "Top with fresh basil, a drizzle of olive oil, and a pinch of salt."
---

## Tips

> Let the dough come to room temperature for at least 30 minutes before stretching. Cold dough fights back!

![Fresh pizza on a cutting board](/images/classic_pizza.jpg)

This pizza pairs beautifully with a simple arugula salad dressed in lemon and olive oil.

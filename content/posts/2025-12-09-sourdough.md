# Tartine bead

Process notes
- Ambient temperature around 65
- Used water temperature of 84 for initial mix
- Autolys for 30 minutes
- Didn't measure levain, might not be 100% hydration
- Didn't smell/taste levain
- Used 450g white flour, 50g rye
- Set toaster oven to proof at 120 for 2 minutes and placed dough inside
- Dough temperature at 30minutes was 77. Reset toaster oven to 120 for 1min
- Fold 2 82, fold 3 80, final dough temperature was 75
- Didn't take photo of it in bowl, had some definite activity. Was able to fully release from bowl w/o ripping
- Did a gentle preshape, covered, and waited 20m

Result
- Good flavor
- Modest rise, not dense but not as light as I would like
- Crumb was still too moist
- No burning on the bottom, adding cast iron underneath appeared to help
